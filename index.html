About Us...

Intelligent Concepts provides graphic presentation services for the corporate events industry. We are based outside of Philadelphia, PA and provide our services to all parts of the world.

Speaker support is a key element of any meeting and that is our specialty.  We have been in the corporate events industry since 1990 and we have specialized in graphic presentation services since 1999.

We have a freelance list of over 20 operators who are based throughout the United States.
